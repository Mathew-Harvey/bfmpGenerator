Create a customized Biofouling Management Plan in minutes. Simply fill out the vessel details below, and we'll produce a plan you can download or print—helping you meet regulatory requirements and protect our oceans.

Get Started

Generate Your BFMP

A Biofouling Management Plan (BFMP) describes the steps your vessel takes to prevent invasive aquatic species from hitching a ride on your hull and niche areas. By completing this form, you’ll produce a professional plan that meets the IMO and Australian biosecurity guidelines. Remember to keep this document onboard and review it regularly.

If provided, your vessel's photo will appear on the cover page of the Plan.

Need Help?

Vessel Name & IMO:
Required by all maritime authorities to uniquely identify your ship.

Plan Date & Review Date:
The date you create/approve the plan and when you’ll review or renew it.

Operating Areas & Speed:
Helps determine how quickly fouling might accumulate based on local waters, climates, or stationary periods.

Why a BFMP?
Biofouling can increase fuel costs and spread invasive species. Many ports now demand a BFMP at entry.
